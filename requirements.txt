MJ TAILORING & TRAINING CENTER
Customer Measurement & Tailoring Management Mobile Application
Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
Version: 1.0
---
1. Project Overview
Project Name
MJ Tailoring Customer Management System (MJ-TCMS)
Client
MJ Tailoring and Training Center
Platform
- Android Mobile Application
- Future Web Dashboard (Optional)
Purpose
The application is designed to digitally manage customer information, body measurements, tailoring orders, payment records, product images, and collection reminders.
The system replaces manual measurement books with a secure and searchable digital database that is simple enough for everyday use by tailoring staff.
---
2. Project Objectives
The application shall enable staff to:
- Register new customers.
- Store customer measurements permanently.
- Record tailoring orders.
- Capture customer fabric/product images.
- Track payment status.
- Monitor collection dates.
- Receive reminders for due collections.
- Search customers quickly.
- Print customer measurement sheets.

18. User Interface Design Principles
The application should be:
- Very simple and easy to learn.
- Large buttons for quick operation.
- Minimal typing with dropdown menus where possible.
- Mobile-friendly.
- Fast even on low-end Android devices.
- Offline-first with optional cloud synchronization.
- Clean, modern, and professional.
---
19. Expected Benefits
- Eliminates paper measurement books.
- Prevents loss of customer records.
- Speeds up customer service.
- Improves payment tracking.
- Ensures timely order collection through reminders.
- Provides professional printable measurement records.
- Enhances customer satisfaction.
- Supports future business growth and expansion.
